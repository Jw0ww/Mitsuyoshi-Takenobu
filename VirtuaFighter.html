Virtua Fighter

One of his more underrated works that often goes under his radar. Unique character songs for the Virtua Fighter series that Mitsuyoshi provided his vocals on. Quite a departure from his typical works and highlights his dynamic and diverse vocal range. Jeffry's theme has recently been featured in the anime series: Hi Score Girls II
