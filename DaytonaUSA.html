Daytona USA

The arcade game that started it all, arguably one of his most memorable and iconic works to date. Easily one of his works that has cemeneted his status as a legend within Sega. The game would not be what it is without his vocal work on the soundtrack. Home versions of Daytona USA even included a Karaoke mode.
