Mitsuyoshi Takenobu (光吉 猛修) is a Japanese musician and businessman, and renowned Sega of Japan composer, known for producing some of Sega's most memorable soundtracks, and for personally acting as a vocalist on a number of critically-acclaimed arcade games - most notably his excitable and passionate singing style featured in Daytona USA.
